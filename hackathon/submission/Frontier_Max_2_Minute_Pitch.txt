Frontier Max is the decision and provenance layer for AI model selection. We make benchmark evidence interpretable, then actionable. In one line: from benchmark to runtime.

Here is the same coding frontier under two real workloads. I enter, “Fix a flaky authentication test while I pair.” Gemini reads the intent and proposes our interactive policy. [1:05–1:28 | Trust and technical design]

Gemini interprets semantics; it does not invent the route. A versioned manifest owns the executable decision. Frontier Max keeps the benchmark source and transformation visible, requests a sticky OpenRouter session for OpenCode, and writes a content-free local receipt. We do not proxy prompts, repositories, keys, diffs, or model responses.
